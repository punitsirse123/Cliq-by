Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 30, 2026. Details on how we collect, process, and secure user information.

Cliqby website is owned and operated by Cliqby Commerce, which acts as the data controller of your personal data.

We have adopted this Privacy Policy, which determines how we process the information collected by Cliqby, providing the exact reasons why we must collect certain personal data about you. Therefore, you must read this Privacy Policy before using the Cliqby website. We take care of your personal data and undertake to guarantee its absolute confidentiality and security.

1. Personal Information We Collect

When you visit Cliqby, we automatically collect certain information about your device, including details regarding your web browser, IP address, timezone, and some of the cookies installed on your device. Additionally, as you browse the Site, we collect details about the individual web pages or products you view, what websites or search terms referred you to the Site, and how you interact with the Site. We refer to this automatically-collected details as “Device Information.”

Moreover, we might collect the personal details you provide to us (including but not limited to Name, Surname, Address, email, phone, etc.) during form submissions or consultations to be able to fulfill any service agreements.

2. Why Do We Process Your Data?

Our top priority is customer data security, and, as such, we may process only minimal user details, only as much as is absolutely necessary to maintain the website and ensure safe operational standards. Information collected automatically is used solely to identify potential cases of abuse and establish statistical data regarding website usage. This statistical details is not otherwise aggregated in such a way that it would identify any particular individual user of the system.

You can visit the website without telling us who you are or revealing any information by which someone could identify you as a specific, identifiable individual. If, however, you wish to use some of the website’s features, claim a free audit, apply for careers, or submit a consultation request, you will provide personal data to us, such as your email, first name, last name, phone number, and store URL.

3. Your Rights

If you are a European resident, you have the following rights related to your personal data:

  • The right to be informed.
  • The right of access.
  • The right to rectification.
  • The right to erasure (right to be forgotten).
  • The right to restrict processing.
  • The right to data portability.
  • The right to object to processing.
  • Rights in relation to automated decision-making and profiling.

If you would like to exercise any of these rights, please contact us through the contact coordinates provided below.

4. Information Security

We secure information you provide on computer servers in a controlled, secure environment, protected from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. We maintain reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect against unauthorized access, use, modification, and data leakage in our control. However, no data transmission over the Internet or wireless networks can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

5. Legal Disclosure

We will disclose any information we collect, use, or receive if required or permitted by law, such as to comply with a subpoena or similar legal process, and when we believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, protect your safety or the safety of others, investigate fraud, or respond to a government request.
