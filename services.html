What We Do

Tailored E-Commerce Growth

We build data-driven strategies designed to scale marketplace rankings, capture high-intent customers, and maximize net profitability.

SERVICE 01

Amazon & Marketplace Advertising

Dominate marketplace search pages with scientific PPC bid architectures. We manage and scale campaigns across Amazon, Flipkart, and Blinkit, targeting buyers when commercial intent is highest.

  • Advanced keyword match type harvesting & bid pacing rules
  • Immersive Sponsored Brand, Video, and DSP setups
  • Hourly campaign monitoring to limit budget waste

Why it matters:

Marketplace PPC is the fastest way to feed the catalog ranking loops, establishing sales velocity that drives organic page-one indexing.

SERVICE 02

Meta Ads Scaling

Accelerate your direct-to-consumer (DTC) sales funnel via robust Meta ad campaigns. We structure pixel integrations, lookalike models, and dynamic catalog ads to capture buyer interest across Facebook and Instagram.

  • High-converting dynamic catalog and collection ads
  • Precision retargeting funnels that recover cart drop-offs
  • Advanced conversion API setups for perfect tracking data

Why it matters:

Meta Ads build visual brand desire, driving qualified buyers to your Shopify storefront or Amazon listings to create mass demand.

SERVICE 03

Google Ads & Performance Max

Intercept commercial searches when shoppers are ready to buy. We manage Google Search campaigns, optimize Google Merchant Center feeds, and orchestrate AI-driven Performance Max (PMax) layouts.

  • Google Merchant Feed optimization for perfect catalog indexing
  • Smart PMax asset groups and budget scaling systems
  • High-ROI Google Search and Shopping campaign structures

Why it matters:

Google captures high-intent searches. Inbound traffic from Google Search and Shopping delivers exceptionally clean conversions.

SERVICE 04

Shopify Setup & Development

Deploy professional, ultra-fast Shopify storefronts. We handle theme customizations, page layout designs, custom app integrations, checkout speed optimizations, and tracking pixel installations.

  • Fully responsive, visual-rich Shopify storefront designs
  • App integrations (reviews, inventory, email lists) and checkout logic
  • Speed optimizations that lower page bounce rates

Why it matters:

Your storefront is your home. A professional, clean, and rapid Shopify setup drives customer trust and maximizes conversions.

SERVICE 05

E-Commerce Organic SEO

Build long-term authority across search engine grids. We perform semantic keyword research, index optimizations, and catalog schema configurations to scale organic placement.

  • Deep semantic search mapping vs leading catalog competitors
  • Meta structures and Structured Data Schema optimization for Google
  • Marketplace SEO alignment that captures organic filters

Why it matters:

Organic clicks cost nothing. Safe organic page-one indexation lowers Blended Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) for sustainable growth.

SERVICE 06

Conversion Rate Optimization (CRO)

Identify and plug purchase leaks in your shopping funnel. We utilize session analytics, A/B landing page testing, and product layout adjustments to turn passive traffic into active checkouts.

  • Store checkout speed and UX friction minimization
  • Heat-map and user recording evaluations to discover leaks
  • High-ROI A/B testing of pricing variables and CTAs

Why it matters:

Doubling your conversion rate halves your customer acquisition cost, instantly unlocking wider profits to scale your campaigns.
